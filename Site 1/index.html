ABOUT

THE ULTIMATE
WORKSHOP EXPERIENCE

Our goal is to start your training so you can be highly employable and sought after within the Canadian movie + television industry.

You will emerge from your workshop(s) with real, practical knowledge, skills and experience which you can take to your next production.

When you are better, we all are better!

WHO IS THIS FOR?

This workshop is for beginners and intermediates in the camera department, as well as gaffers, directors and producers that want to understand how the camera department works. This is great film networking with the possibility of film gigs.

PROFESSIONAL EQUIPMENT

Full Steadicam and camera packages supplied with professional cameras, prime lenses, zoom lenses, remote wireless focus units, and HD video transmitters

HANDS-ON EXPERIENCE

Learn practical on-set skills and knowledge that will take you to a higher level in camera department operations

INDUSTRY NETWORKING

Connect with professionals and get real opportunities. Many students get hired after completing workshops.

CAREER ADVANCEMENT
