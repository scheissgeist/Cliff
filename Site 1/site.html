WORK IN FILM  



Our goal is to start your training so you to be highly employable and sought after within the Canadian movie + television industry. You will emerge from your workshop(s) with real, practical knowledge, skills and experience with which you can take to your next production.

When you are better, we all are better!


WHO IS THIS FOR?
GET HIRED IN THE INDUSTRY!

​Head instructor Cliff Hokansan has trained a large amount of individuals over the past 20 years who are successfully working in camera departments as focus pullers, Steadicam operators, and camera assistants in Vancouver's busy movie + television market. ​
This workshop is for beginners and intermediates in the camera department, as well as gaffers, directors and producers that want to understand how the camera department works. Cliff gives you on-set skills and practical knowledge that will take you to a higher level. This is great film networking with the possibility of film gigs.

INTRO + ADVANCED
CAMERA DEVELOPMENT WORKSHOPS
​
Want to work in film or just up your game? Whether you are new to the business or already have the foundations, this weekend-long Film Crew Workshop in Vancouver offers instruction on beginner and advanced camera operating skills, 1st and 2nd Assistant Camera (AC), focus pulling, as well as Steadicam Operator. Build your skills, gain practical hands-on skills, and above all – get into the game.

​LEARN TO BECOME OR ADVANCE YOUR SKILLS AS A:

  • Camera Operator
  • Focus Puller
  • 1st camera assistant
  • 2nd camera assistant
  • Gimbal Operator
  • Steadicam Operator
  • Car mounts set up
  • Jib arm remote head operating
  • Lighting for Documentry interview
  • Lighting for a motion picture scene
  • BONUS: How to Shoot a Music Video
